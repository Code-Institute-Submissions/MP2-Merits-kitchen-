Merit's Kitchen

Merit's kitchen is a home made food restaurant which is runned by Merit Thomsen. Merit cooks every day for her 3 boys and husband. She knows how stressful it can be planning dinner, after long, tiring day at work. Merit's kitchen is here to make it easier, so you have more time to enjoy with your family and friends.
Merit uses products from local farms, handpicked the raw ingredients, and then prepare them the same day in her kitchen.
Make an order from our Menu and get it fresh made in the same evening!
